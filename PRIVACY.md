# Privacy Policy — Solvronix Desk

Last updated: June 15, 2026

## What We Collect
Solvronix Desk is a self-hosted Frappe theme. It runs entirely on 
your own server. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal 
data from your ERPNext installation.

## Your Data
All data entered into your ERPNext system stays on your server. 
Solvronix has no access to your data.

## Theme Settings
Company name, logo, and color preferences set in Theme Settings 
are stored in your own database on your own server.

## Third Parties
Solvronix Desk does not integrate with any third-party analytics, 
tracking, or data collection services.
